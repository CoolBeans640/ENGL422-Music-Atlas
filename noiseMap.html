Space Grunge

Space Grunge is a microgenre of music that was a precursor to Grungegaze. It was a genre that mixed Space Rock and Grunge! There is not a lot of good examples of the genre as most modern Shoegaze acts/Grungegaze acts can be labeled space grunge. From what I have learned, only Failure and Hum were truly Space Grunge, despite this, it is important to mention this microgenre.

Slowcore

Some saw Grunge as too mainstream found it to be abrasive and mainstream. This spawned Slowcore, aka Sadcore. Slowcore is a small quiet microgenre of rock that focuses on slowed tempos, quiet vocals and minimization of instruments. This is important genre to mention due to the fact it also is associated with the later shoegaze resurgance.
